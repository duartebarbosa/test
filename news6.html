Concert for children with autism and their families

  • Concert for children with autism and their families

By: Admin | Posted on: 21 Aug 2015

We are collaborating with Foundation Papageno and we performed a concert for children with autism and their families in Heel (Limburg).

We spent an evening at the Wigwamvallei in Heel were our quartet performed the Four Seasons of Vivaldi with the solo of Meri Khojayan and the narration of Veronika Juhasz. The audience included children with autism and their families. Members of the families thanked us at the end of the concert saying that they had never seen their children so concentrated and quiet.
