Stichting Muziek voor een Leven has three active projects.
We organize educational concerts for children, therapeutic concerts for ill children and regular concerts for the elderly. Each type of concert has a different approach, please discover more here!

Children

We organize educational concerts in schools. During these concerts there is a classical quartet performing and a narrator creating the verbal interaction with the children. It is an opportunity for the children to listen to a live concert, to learn more about music and to arouse interest in classical music.

Ill children

We organize concerts in hospitals and rehabilitation centers. These concerts are adapted to each situation and have a therapeutically goal. We want to give these children moments of happiness trough music during their recovery period.

Elderly

We organize concerts at retirement houses. The concerts for elderly people have the goal to connect the young generation with the elderly generation. These concerts always bring a big smile and moments of happiness!
